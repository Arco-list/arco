# Arco Pricing Strategy

## Core Principle

**Publishing = free. Visibility = paid.**

Architects create the projects that power Arco. They should never pay to publish. Professionals who benefit from being credited on those projects pay to grow their visibility.

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Free (€0/month)

**Target:** Everyone getting started — architects publishing projects and professionals exploring the platform.

| Feature | Included |
|---|---|
| Published projects | Unlimited |
| Contributor projects | 3 |
| Company page | Basic |
| Team management | — |
| Portfolio analytics | — |
| Priority in search | — |

**Why free works:** Removes all friction for architects to publish. Professionals get a taste of being credited and see the value before upgrading. The 3-project credit limit creates natural upgrade pressure.

---

### Portfolio (€39/month annual · €49/month monthly)

**Target:** Established professionals who have enough work but want to look professional online. They use Arco as their portfolio — not to generate leads.

| Feature | Included |
|---|---|
| Published projects | Unlimited |
| Contributor projects | Unlimited |
| Company page | Full |
| Team management | ✓ |
| Portfolio analytics | ✓ |
| Priority in search | — |

**Value proposition:** "Showcase your best work." These users pay for how they look, not what they get. Portfolio is a vanity product — it's about professional pride and credibility.

**Pricing rationale:** €39/month anchors the real price. ~20% monthly premium (€49) nudges toward annual commitments. Early-bird offer: first ~50 users get €19/month locked for 12 months.

**Upgrade trigger:** When a professional hits the 3-credit limit and wants more projects on their portfolio.

---

### Growth (€59/month annual · €79/month monthly)

**Target:** Professionals actively looking for new clients. They want Arco to bring them business, not just display their work.

| Feature | Included |
|---|---|
| Published projects | Unlimited |
| Contributor projects | Unlimited |
| Company page | Full |
| Team management | ✓ |
| Portfolio analytics | ✓ |
| Priority in search | ✓ |
| Featured in category | ✓ |
| Lead insights | ✓ |

**Value proposition:** "Grow your business." These users pay for ROI — better positioning means more inquiries. The price should scale with value delivered.

**Pricing rationale:** €59/month anchors clearly above Portfolio. ~25% monthly premium (€79) incentivizes annual. Still well under Checkatrade (£100+/month) for lead generation.

**Upgrade trigger:** When a professional realizes they're getting views but not enough inquiries, and wants better positioning.

## Revenue Evolution

### Phase 1: Build Supply (now)
- Architects publish for free → builds project inventory
- Professionals join via credits → builds professional directory
- All features effectively free → maximize adoption
- **Revenue: €0** (investment phase)

### Phase 2: Monetize Demand (next)
- Introduce Portfolio tier → professionals pay for unlimited credits + analytics
- Keep publishing free for everyone
- **Revenue: Portfolio subscriptions (~€39-49 × active professionals)**

### Phase 3: Performance Pricing (future)
- Introduce Growth tier with search priority
- Start with flat fee (€59-79/month)
- Evolve to **base + per-lead** model: "€59/month base + €5 per qualified inquiry"
- This scales revenue with value delivered (Checkatrade model)
- **Revenue: Grows with search volume and lead generation**

### Phase 4: Category Leadership (long-term)
- Featured placements in category pages
- Sponsored project positions in discover
- Geographic exclusivity (premium pricing for being the only featured architect in a city)
- **Revenue: High-margin premium placements**

## Key Decisions

### Why not charge architects?
Architects create the supply. Without projects, there's nothing for professionals to be credited on, and nothing for homeowners to discover. Charging architects would slow down the most critical growth lever.

### Why differentiate on publish vs. contribute?
A company can be both — they publish their own projects AND get credited on others'. The value exchange is different:
- **Publishing** = you create value for the platform → free
- **Contributing** = you extract value from others' content → monetizable

### Why start with 2 tiers, not 3?
Growth tier needs search volume to deliver value. Launching it before there's meaningful traffic would feel like charging for nothing. Launch it when professionals can actually see ROI from better positioning.

### Why €39/€49 for Portfolio?
€39/month (annual) anchors the real price while staying affordable for any established studio. The €49 monthly option adds a ~20% flexibility premium that nudges toward annual commitments. Early-bird pricing (€19/month for first ~50 users) rewards early adopters while keeping the anchor at €39.

### Why €59/€79 for Growth?
Clear price separation from Portfolio signals a different tier of value. €79/month (monthly) is still well under Checkatrade (£100+/month) for lead generation. The ~25% annual discount (€59) incentivizes commitment. Room to evolve into a per-lead model as search volume grows.
