Locked spec — badge rules (all formats)

Asset: /public/arco-og-badge.png (312×312 master; source at docs/brand/arco-og-badge-source.png).

Position: flush with the bottom edge of the canvas, 3.3% off the right edge. Pinned in percentages so it lands in the same visual spot regardless of aspect ratio.

Size: 14% of the canvas's shorter side — a single rule for every ratio. CSS: width: min(14cqw, 14cqh); aspect-ratio: 1 on a card with container-type: size. Resolves to:
landscape 1200×630 — 88px (short side = height)
square 1080×1080 — 151px
portrait 1080×1350 — 151px (short side = width)
story 1080×1920 — 151px
pin 1000×1500 — 140px

Protected region: crops should avoid placing a busy focal point in the bottom-right region. Choose hero photos with ground / hardscape / landscape below the architecture so the badge seats cleanly.

Open in Safari or Chrome. Resize the window — every card resizes proportionally and the badge stays pinned to its percentage-based anchor.
